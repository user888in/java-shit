# Privacy Policy for AquaTrack

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## Overview

AquaTrack is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We collect NO personal data.**

AquaTrack is a privacy-first application that stores all data locally on your device. We do not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your usage
- Send data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Require account creation
- Access your contacts, camera, or other device features (except notifications with your permission)

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:
- Water intake logs
- Exercise records
- Standing break logs
- Settings and preferences
- Streaks and statistics

## Data Control

You have complete control over your data:
- **Export:** Download all your data as JSON anytime
- **Import:** Restore data from a previous export
- **Delete:** Clear all data by clearing browser storage
- **Backup:** Automatic backups are created locally before updates

## Permissions

AquaTrack may request the following permissions:

### Notifications (Optional)
- **Purpose:** Send reminders to drink water and take breaks
- **Control:** Can be enabled/disabled in settings
- **Data:** No data is sent; notifications are generated locally

### Storage (Required)
- **Purpose:** Save your water intake, exercises, and settings
- **Control:** Essential for app functionality
- **Data:** Stored only on your device

## Third-Party Services

AquaTrack uses:
- **Google Fonts:** For typography (loaded from Google's CDN)
- No other third-party services, analytics, or tracking

## Children's Privacy

AquaTrack does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app can be used by anyone, and all data remains on the user's device.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, please create an issue in the project repository.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your data (via export feature)
- Delete all your data (clear browser storage)
- Control notification permissions
- Use the app without providing any personal information

---

**Summary:** AquaTrack is completely private. Your data never leaves your device.
